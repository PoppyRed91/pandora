Titans Prometheus and Epimetheus were given the task of creating creatures on Earth.

Epimetheus crafted animals, giving each unique skills. Prometheus shaped man but had no gifts to give.

Prometheus wanted to help mankind and asked Zeus for fire, but Zeus refused. Fire was only for the Gods.

Defying Zeus, Prometheus gave fire to humans

Zeus wanted to punish Prometeus and humans for taking fire from the Gods.

So he ordered the gods to create Pandora, who was endowed with gifts of beauty, wisdom, kindness and curiosity.

Pandora was given to Epimetheus as a wife, despite Prometheus' warning to his brother not to accept gifts from Zeus. Epimetheus, captivated by Pandora’s beauty, married her. As a wedding gift, Zeus gave Pandora a box but warned her never to open it.

Even though she tried to resist it...

...Pandora’s curiosity got the better of her, and she opened the box

Unimaginable things were hidden in the box, waiting to be released into the world...

The box unleashed all the evils of the world: greed, envy, hatred, pain, disease, hunger, poverty, war, and death.

Pandora was terrified and she closed the box and dropped it...
