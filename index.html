Pandora's box

Prometheus and Epimetheus, Titans who supported Zeus during his battle with the Titans, were given the task of creating creatures on Earth. Epimetheus crafted animals, giving each unique skills and protection. Prometheus shaped man but had no gifts left to give. Prometheus wanted to help mankind and asked Zeus for fire, but Zeus refused. Fire was only for the Gods. Defying Zeus, Prometheus took the fire and gave it to the humans.
